FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Are all watches on Malenia authentic? +
Yes, absolutely. Every single watch we carry is rigorously authenticated before being listed. We source directly from authorized distributors and provide a certificate of authenticity with every purchase.
How long does delivery take? +
Standard delivery within Dhaka takes 1-2 business days. Nationwide delivery takes 3-5 business days. All orders are shipped in our premium gift packaging with tracking information provided.
What is your return policy? +
We offer a full 30-day return policy, no questions asked. If for any reason you are not completely satisfied, contact us within 30 days of delivery and we will arrange a full refund or exchange.
Do you offer corporate / bulk ordering? +
Yes! We have special pricing for corporate gifts and bulk orders. Please contact us via the form above or email us at corporate@maleniawatches.com and our team will get back to you within 24 hours.
Can I get gift wrapping and a personalized message? +
Absolutely! All Malenia orders come in our signature luxury box. You can add a personalized message card during checkout at no extra cost.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept bKash, Nagad, Visa, Mastercard, and cash on delivery (COD) for orders within Dhaka. We also offer easy installment plans on selected products.
